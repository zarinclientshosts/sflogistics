What is Truck Dispatch Service?

Truck dispatch services assist truck drivers and owner-operators in managing the load booking and administrative tasks involved in running a trucking company.
While some dispatchers focus solely on load booking, services like Genius Dispatch extend to include invoice management and handling detention requests, offering comprehensive support to streamline operations.
